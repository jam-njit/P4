    Welcome to Rivengardian Tales, where it’s your tale unfolding. Step into a living world of kingdoms, secrets, and legends shaped by your choices. Your story begins here. Step into a vast, living world shaped by ancient magic, shifting alliances, and the choices you make. In this story-driven RPG, you play as a rising hero caught in the middle of a growing conflict that threatens the balance between realms. Explore sprawling lands filled with mysteries, form bonds with unforgettable companions, and battle powerful foes using a deep, strategic combat system. As you journey across enchanted forests, forgotten ruins, and bustling cities, every decision influences your path — whether you become a savior, a conqueror, or something in between. Craft your playstyle by mastering unique classes, upgrading abilities, and forging rare equipment. Engage in meaningful quests, uncover hidden lore, and experience a world that evolves based on your actions. The fate of the realm rests in your hands. Will you rise to protect it… or reshape it?

    Our Vision

    Our goal is to create a world that feels alive — where your decisions matter, factions change, and the world evolves over time. We want players to feel like their presence genuinely shapes history.

    Team Canvas

    Team Canvas is an independent game development studio built on creativity, passion, and flexibility. Founded by a small group of dedicated developers, the team works collaboratively—each member contributing at their own pace while balancing school, work, and life. Despite operating part-time, Team Canvas aims to deliver polished, memorable game experiences shaped by strong storytelling, unique art styles, and innovative gameplay systems. Our current projects explore worlds filled with rich lore, strategic combat, and immersive design, including Rivengardian Tales, a growing universe crafted with care and depth. Team Canvas focuses on creating games that resonate emotionally, spark imagination, and bring players into vibrant, handcrafted worlds. As we expand our skills and portfolio, our mission remains simple: Create meaningful games, support passionate creators, and build worlds worth exploring.
