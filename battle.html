    Reactive Turn Based Battle

    This is our take on turn-based battle systems. It takes the strategic elements of turn-based battles, as well as the urgency of real-time battles. Players will have to pay attention mid-turns by performing several actions such as blocking, countering, and switching members to perform special actions.

    Multi-action Turns

    A turn doesn't end as soon as an action is performed. Everyone will get the chance to do more in one turn. However, the risk of getting countered by a monster becomes greater as the length of a turn increases.

    Tactical Positioning

    Move your character around the battlefield to avoid attacks.
