    An ALIVE World

    Rivengardian Tales features a dynamic world that is facilitated by the ALIVE System. This takes elements of the world and dictates how they interact with one another, creating scenarios that will change the world on varying scales.

    The world dynamic, and no day is ever the same.

    Life-like NPCs

    Rivengard is home to many unique NPCs. Just like you, they will live their own lives. Whether you are there to witness it or not, NPCs will continue to forge their own tales.

    Forge Relationships

    Befriend these NPCs, or become their enemies. Rivengardian Tales gives you the freedom to Choose how you forge these relationships.
