    • Classes

    Warrior

    The warrior is the tank of the party. They can withstand strong attacks, and is capable of catching the attention of monsters a lot easier than other classes. Combine different Weapon Arts in one turn, each with their own special modifiers to turn the tide of battle.

    Mage

    Mages can deal massive amounts of damage through the use of mana. Combine different incantations to conjure a very specific spell. Whether it is to eliminate their foes, or manipulate the battlefield around them, a mage's versatility may determine the outcome of a battle.

    Bard

    Bards don't deal much damage, nor are strong enough to take some. However, they can play music that touches the soul of those who hear it. These musical pieces buff allies, and curse foes.
